Life expectancy at birth indicates the number of years a newborn infant would live if prevailing patterns of mortality at the time of its birth were to stay the same throughout its life.
Of course, these patterns change during a person's life and a lot of other parameters should be taken into account for a more accurate result.
This is not the point of this app.

What's the point then?

The idea is simply to remind us that time goes by, that we are going to die one day and that maybe we should do these things we want to do - before it's too late.
It is really just a motivational tool for me, as well as a fun data representation ;)

The life expectancy data used in this app was downloaded from The World Bank.
